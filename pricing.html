FAQ

Common pricing questions

What's included in the price?

Everything. Design, development, copywriting support, SEO, analytics setup, domain/hosting configuration, and post-launch support. No hidden fees.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. We offer 50/50 payment: 50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery. For Scale plans, we offer 3-part payment.

What if I need changes after the project?

30 days of free support is included. After that, our retainer plan at ₹50,000/month covers unlimited small updates and optimizations.

Do you charge for hosting?

No. We set up your site on Vercel or Netlify (free tier covers most startups). Domain registration is a separate ~₹1,000/year cost.

How does the money-back guarantee work?

After the Discovery phase (Day 1–3), if you're not satisfied with the wireframes and direction, request a full refund. We process it within 5 business days.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! Many clients start with Starter and upgrade to Growth when they close their seed round. We offer a loyalty discount for returning clients.
