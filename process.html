About the process

How many revision rounds are included?

Starter includes 2 rounds, Growth includes 3 rounds. Each round covers all pages. Most projects are approved in round 1–2.

How do we communicate during the sprint?

Dedicated Slack channel + daily async updates. We also schedule 2 video check-ins per week.

What if the scope changes mid-sprint?

We lock scope at kickoff but allow minor adjustments. Major changes are discussed and quoted separately — no surprises.

Can I see work-in-progress during the sprint?

Absolutely. You have real-time Figma access and staging site access from day 7 onwards.

What happens after the 14 days?

30 days of included support, then optional retainer packages starting at ₹50,000/month for ongoing updates and optimization.
